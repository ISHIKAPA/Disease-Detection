Heart Disease Prediction

Welcome to the Heart Disease Prediction Project



"Predicting Hearts, Saving Lives." Heart disease is one of the leading causes of death, but early detection can make all the difference. Using advanced machine learning, our model predicts heart disease risk based on health indicators like age, cholesterol, and blood pressure, empowering individuals and healthcare professionals to make informed decisions.

"Proactive Health, Better Outcomes." Our tool provides a risk score to help identify individuals at risk, enabling timely interventions for healthier hearts. By bridging the gap between medical expertise and data science, we offer a proactive approach to heart disease prevention.

"Your Heart Health, Our Priority." Join us in the fight against heart disease and take the first step toward a healthier heart today!
